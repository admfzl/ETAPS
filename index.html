Who We Are

Since our inception in 2012, EPOMS has grown by leaps and bounds, providing Operations & Maintenance solutions from pre-production to post-production activities in the oil & gas industry.

Our internal talent is our intrinsic strength and main competitive advantage. All our employees are carefully selected for their experiential calibre and expertise to deliver innovative training solutions that accommodate current and future industry demands.

EPOMS Training Academy accelerates human capital development through experiential learning, delivering customised niche programmes anchored on world-class facilities and industry-experienced instructors.
